The short version: DETENT collects nothing about you. It has no
internet connection, no accounts, and no servers. Everything you
enter stays on your phone.

---

What data the app stores

DETENT stores your rolls, frames, gear, and settings in a local
database on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere.

If you enable GPS on a roll, the app will ask for location
permission and attach coordinates to frames you log. Those
coordinates are stored locally in the same database — they are
never transmitted, uploaded, or shared.

What the app does not do

- No analytics or usage tracking of any kind
- No advertising
- No crash reporting sent to external servers
- No accounts or login
- No cloud sync
- No network requests (the app is fully offline)

Backups

If you use the backup feature, the app creates a .DETENT file on
your device. You choose where it goes via the Android share sheet
— the app does not send it anywhere automatically.

Third-party services

None. The app has no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated version
will be included in that release.
